All of Shaquille O’Neal’s children maintain a great relationship with their father, despite him being a single dad. While many would expect the big man to have a turbulent relationship with Myles, that surely isn’t the case. Not only does the 26-year-old have a great repo with the Los Angeles Lakers legend, but he also carries his last name. Now a runway model, Myles seems to share a similar bond with his new stepfather, after he was seen giving love to the Houston-based pastor.

Shaquille O’Neal is the proud father of six children that he shares with two of his ex-partners. Shaq and Shaunie were married for 9 years before calling it quits, and it is with her that Shaq fathered 4 children. The star of ‘Basketball Wives‘ also had a son from her previous relationship, named Myles, who the 4x NBA champion legally adopted after marrying Shaunie in 2002.

Shaquille O’Neal’s son shares love with new stepfather

In early June, music producer DJ Khaled announced his inaugural ‘We the Best Foundation Golf Classic‘ sponsored by the Jordan brand. Hosted at the Miami Beach Golf Club, the affair saw many A-list celebrities being invited. With the likes of Diddy, Quavo, Timbaland, and Myles’ new stepfather, Keion Henderson, all making the guest list. The Houston-based pastor, who married Shaunie in 2022, was an unexpected face in the event, as his stepson showed him some love on Instagram.

DJ Khaled shared glimpses of the event on his Instagram as he showcased his newest creation to the world. During one such video, Timbaland can be seen speaking to Khaled about their golf drives as Hassan Whiteside and Keion Henderson look on. The Grammy-nominated producer then goes on to point at the pastor as he states, “He can hit the beat, K can hit the beat.”

Posting a screenshot of the video featuring the three, Myles captioned the image “This is an insane picture“. Tagging Keion in it, the young man showed love to his stepfather, as he enjoyed the event hosted by Khaled and the Jordan brand.

Is Keion and Shaunie Henderson a perfect pair?

Engaged in the latter months of 2021, Keion and Shaunie Henderson married each other at the Aurora Anguilla Resort and Golf Club in the summer of 2022. Attended by all of Shaq’s children, the ceremony was a small but beautiful one featuring a lot of smiles. A year later and the couple discussed their relationship with both of them stating, “It’s been easy“.

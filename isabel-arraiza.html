Outer Range Actress Isabel Arraiza Wiki | Bio

Isabel Arraiza (born on August 2, 1990) is a 31 years old Boricua actress. who has been gaining fame with her outstanding performance in the 2021 released movie “The Little Things,” and since April 15, Isabel’s stardom has skyrocketed after landing as Maria Olivares in Brian Watkins’ mini-series “Outer Range.”

The very talented Josh Brolin plays the role of the lead character in this show. Other actors involved in making this movie were Noah Reid, Olive Abercrombie, Tom Pelphrey, and others.

From her agent, we learned that she is currently involved in the production of a Simon Hacker movie called “Zoo.” The film will be ready by the end of 2022, and we can expect its release in the mid of 2023.

She was in the first year of her training program when she cleared auditions of “Más Que el agua” and got to perform the role of Carol.

The experience of the first acting job was pleasant, and that fueled Isabel’s motive of becoming an actress. One year after completing acting training, Arraiza got roles in 2 shows, namely “Elementary” and “The Oath.”

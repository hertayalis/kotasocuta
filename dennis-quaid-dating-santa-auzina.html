Dennis Quaid's new 30-year-old girlfriend revealed

July 2024 · 2 minute read

    It seems Dennis Quaid has a new lady in his life. The 62-year-old actor is said to be dating 30-year-old model Santa Auzina –and she is not shy about showcasing their blossoming relationship. The blonde beauty has shared a number of Instagram snapshots of the couple together in the last month, including one with the hashtag "#mylove".

    The pair have been spending time together in Montreal, Canada, where Dennis is currently filming his TV show .

    Since then there have been a number of pictures, including one taken at the Notre-Dame Basilica just last week in which Santa refers to Dennis as her soulmate. "#notredame #catholic #santita #sundaymass # church # mylove # spiritual #soulmate #teamo #blondie #montreal #canada # amorepuro," she captioned the selfie.

    Dennis's new romance comes two months after his wife Kimberly filed for divorce from the actor. "After careful consideration, we have decided to end our 12-year marriage," the couple said in a joint statement at the time. "The decision was made amicably and with mutual respect toward each other."

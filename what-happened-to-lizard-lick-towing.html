September 2024 · 4 minute read

Lizard Lick Towing was an American reality television show that captured the day-to-day operations of Lizard Lick Towing and Recovery, a repossession company located in Lizard Lick, North Carolina. The show, which was a spin-off of the series “All Worked Up,” aired on truTV and became popular for its colorful cast, dramatic repossessions, and the unique dynamics of running a towing business. However, fans of the show were left wondering what happened to Lizard Lick Towing after it seemingly disappeared from the airwaves. Introduction to Lizard Lick Towing

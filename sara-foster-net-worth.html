Sara Foster Wiki Biography

Sara Michael Foster was born on the 5th February 1981, in Los Angeles, California, USA of Canadian-American descent, and is an actress, but also known for her works as a photo model. As an actress she is best known for the role of Jen Clark in the teen drama series “90210” (2008 – 2013). Foster has been active in the entertainment industry since 1999.

How rich is the actress? It has been reported by authoritative sources that the overall size of Sara Foster’s net worth is as much as $3 million, as of the data presented in late 2016. Modelling and acting are the main sources of her wealth.

To begin with, Sara Foster is the second oldest daughter of Canadian music producer David Foster, the fifteen times Grammy winner who produced such top celebrities as Celine Dion, Barbra Streisand, Mariah Carey, Toni Braxton, Madonna, Whitney Houston and many others. He is one of the most successful producers of the last twenty years. Sara was raised in Los Angeles; her sister Erin Foster is also an actress.

Concerning the professional career of Sara Foster, initially she was a model in the fashion industry. In 2001 she moderated the program “ET” on MTV, an abridged version of the highly successful show broadcast since 1981. In the USA, entertainment and news show “Entertainment Tonight” was broadcast daily, although the program was stopped quite soon after, it still helped Sara Foster to break through in the film business. Subsequently, Sara got the lead role in the action comedy film “D.E.B.S.” (2004) written and directed by Angela Robinson. Then she received a starring role in the film “The Big Bounce” (2004) directed by George Armitage. In 2008, she was in the main cast of romantic comedy film “The Other End of the Line” by James Dodson, and the same year she was invited to appear in a recurring role in the teen drama series “90210” (2008 – 2013) developed by Jeff Judah, Gabe Sachs and Rob Thomas. Meanwhile, she landed the lead role in the horror film written and directed by Andrew Shortell – “Psych 9” (2010) – which premiered at the Screamfest Horror Film Festival. Foster was also seen in the comedy film “Demoted” (2011) directed by J. B. Rogers.

Since 2015, Sara stars in the reality television parody “Barely Famous” aired on VH1; moreover, she is co-creator of the above mentioned show. In addition to other movie and television roles, she has also appeared in the music video “Shape of My Heart” by the Backstreet Boys. To conclude, all the aforementioned engagements have added significant sums to the total size of Sara Foster’s net worth as well as popularity.

Finally, in the personal life of the actress, Foster had relationships with celebrities such as Leonardo DiCaprio. From 2006 she was in relationship with the German professional tennis player Tommy Haas; the two became engaged in 2008 and married in 2010, and the family has two daughters.
